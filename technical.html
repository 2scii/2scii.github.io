A box I designed using CAD to hold an arduino. I realized later that there were not many error margins which led to some issues with accessing the ports.

As part of the Robotics Team, I helped with building and design. Part of the challenge was our limited number of supplies due to shipping delays. I found a creative work around for the hook-conveyor belt system as well as finding an alternative to using wheels for intake and instead using rubber bands.

A fond memory! This was my first ever technical project: building a PC. Honestly building a PC is much easier than it sounds when ordering the parts. The hard part was troubleshooting, finding part compatibility, ensuring the parts had the specs I wanted, and researching case, cooler and fan combos for better air flow.
