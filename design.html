School Agenda Cover - Front & Back 2024

This design reminds me of high school! It was a last minute request right before school ended and I remember the hours flying by as I worked away at the digital art. The hardest part was really getting a good, satisfying idea. My inspiration came from the sunrises next to the floor to ceiling windows in the school's giant multipurpose room. I remember early mornings of volunteer events with stunning pink-orange skies.
